Typing Speed Test

Welcome to the typing speed test. Please click your test option to begin.



This test works by displaying the passage that you have selected first. You will then begin typing in that passage in the box labeled "Type Here." Once you are finished typing press the "Submit Score" button. If you would like to restart, hit the "Restart" button.
The test is assessed by comparing each word in the passage you typed, in order, to the displayed passage. Time between hitting submit and finishing isn't counted. More than one space in between words will not be counted against you. Others factors will also be measured: backspaces used, time not typing, WPM, CPM, Errors.
Features to be added include: selecting word limit, staying above a certain error limit, practicing certain types of diffcultly of words (E, M, H).
